PodScholar Rules

Upload. Listen. Share.

Hello! You've made it to the rules page of PodScholar, here are the desingated rules for the site below.

1. One podcast per published work.

2. 20 Minute Maximum Length Podcast.

3. Podcasters much be an author on the published work.

4. Published works must have a registred DOI#.

5. Audio file must be mp3 or mp4 format to upload.
